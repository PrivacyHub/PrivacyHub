Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about IoT privacy answered

What data does my smart speaker collect?

Smart speakers collect voice commands, ambient audio (when they detect wake words), device usage patterns, and sometimes location data. This information is sent to cloud servers for processing and can be used to improve services or shared with third parties.

How can I stop my smart TV from tracking me?

You can disable ACR (Automatic Content Recognition) in your TV's privacy settings, opt out of data collection in the terms of service, and regularly clear your viewing history. Check our Smart TV guide for step-by-step instructions.

Are my conversations always being recorded?

While devices are always listening for wake words (like "Alexa" or "Hey Google"), they only start recording and sending data to the cloud after detecting these words. However, accidental activations can occur, leading to unintended recordings.

What rights do I have under GDPR?

Under GDPR, you have the right to access your data, request deletion, opt out of data processing, and receive clear information about how your data is used. Smart device manufacturers must comply with these requirements for EU users.
