About Our Research

These surveys are part of an academic research project at Manchester Metropolitan University to evaluate the effectiveness of privacy education platforms. Your responses are anonymous and will only be used to improve privacy awareness resources. No personal identifying information is collected.

All responses are encrypted and handled in accordance with GDPR guidelines.
