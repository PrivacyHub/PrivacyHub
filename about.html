About SmartPrivacyHub

Empowering users to take control of their IoT privacy

Our Mission

SmartPrivacyHub is an educational platform designed to raise awareness about the privacy risks associated with Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Our mission is to empower users to take control of their personal data and make informed decisions regarding the privacy settings of their connected devices, such as smart speakers, smart TVs, smart watches, and other IoT devices.

The Problem

The number of connected IoT devices is projected to reach 39 billion by 2030, yet research shows that 48% of users are completely unaware of the privacy implications of their devices. Many don't realize that smart devices are constantly collecting data about their habits, conversations, and daily routines. This information can be shared with third parties, used for targeted advertising, or exposed in data breaches.

Our Solution

We provide in-depth, user-friendly content through blog posts and guides that explain IoT privacy risks, potential data breaches, and practical steps users can take to protect their privacy. Our content is designed with a user-centered approach, ensuring that even non-technical users can easily understand and apply the information presented.

Key Features

  • Educational Blog: In-depth articles about privacy risks for various IoT devices
  • Privacy Guides: Step-by-step instructions for securing popular devices
  • Research-Based: Content grounded in academic research and industry reports
  • User-Centered Design: Accessible to users with all levels of technical expertise
  • GDPR Compliant: We respect your privacy and collect minimal data

About the Creator

SmartPrivacyHub was created by Asayel Hasan as part of an academic project at Manchester Metropolitan University. The project aims to address the critical gap in user awareness about IoT privacy risks and provide accessible education to help users protect their personal data in an increasingly connected world.
